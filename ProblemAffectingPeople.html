Problems Affect on States

U.S Congress and other states still believe ISIS is a small problem, but the truth is that ISIS actually represents a dangerous new chapter in the global war and it is a threat to the U.S. homeland. 66% of Americans favor fighting Isis and 30% oppose the airstrikes on Isis. 52% of Americans oppose sending troops to Syria and 43% favor sending troops to Syria. 78% of Americans want to make Congress Vote to send military troops too Syria.
